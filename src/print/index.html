After his 61th birthday, Jep Gambardella,
an aging intellectual and one-time writer,
decides to dedicate his life to the search
of a meaning.
On the background of a mysterious and
wonderful Rome,our main character slowly
rediscovers beauty under the monotonous,
empty shell of a lifetime between sinners.
Following a boring night spent between
the sheets of a boring and egocentric rich
woman from Milan, Jep decides to stick on
a quieter quality of life, but several, casual,
meetings will force him to reconsider his
priorities.
A stripper on the verge of breakdown,
the ex-husband of his first love, a bizarre
magician and a living Saint from a far away
reality, will soon allow Jep to discover
his own, personal, Great Beauty.
